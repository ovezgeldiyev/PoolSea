Pulsechain staking pool

Our staking pool allows users to earn a share of the rewards from staking in the Pulsechain network, even if they don't have billions of PLS required to participate individually due to the recent change in the network's consensus protocol. By pooling their resources and staking together, users can remain liquid in stPLS on their wallet and earn extra yields decentralized applications such as GOL.

GOL Stablecoin

GOL is a decentralized stablecoin pegged to the value of gold ounces. It can be obtained through the process of edging, in which other cryptocurrencies (stPLS, PLS, HEX and pHex) are used as collateral, or it can also be purchased on decentralized exchanges such as PoolSwap or PulseX. In the near future, holders of GOL will have the option to exchange their stablecoins for physical gold at a 1:1 ratio in locations including Switzerland, Dubai, Qatar, and the Bahamas, among others. This exchange will be conducted anonymously, with the identities of the participants remaining undisclosed. All exchanges involving GOL will be conducted in a fully legal manner.
